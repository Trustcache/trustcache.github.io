Hobbies

Jailbreak tweaking

    I was introduced to jailbreaking my iPhone as I hated how I could not sideload apps. Jailbreaking is the process of achieving root access to one's iPhone.

    As jailbreaking is extremely powerful, it also allows one to inject code into iOS, more colloquially known as "tweaks". As such, its extremely hard to develop a Jailbreak due to the security that latest iOS has.

    I'm not talented enough to make tweaks, but heres my jailbroken iPhone!

maimai!

    Recently, I picked up a new rhythm game known as maimai! It's an arcade rhythm game thats slowly but surely burning a hole in my pocket! Its a fast pace rhythm game that is made up of a touch screen surrounded by 8 buttons.

    This game requires alot of stamina, which is unique to the game. Only being in this hobby for 2 months, I have gotten exponentially better, rising up to master difficulty and high level songs while at the cost of some money and energy. Shown is my progress in the 2 months of playing!
